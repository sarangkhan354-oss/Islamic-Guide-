Islamic Pillars

Learn about the five pillars of Islam that form the foundation of a Muslim's faith and practice.

Shahada

Declaration of faith in the oneness of God and the prophethood of Muhammad.

Salah

Performing the five daily prayers to strengthen the connection with Allah.

Zakat

Giving charity to those in need, purifying wealth and helping the community.

Sawm

Fasting during the month of Ramadan to develop self-control and devotion.

Hajj

Pilgrimage to Mecca that every Muslim must undertake at least once if able.
